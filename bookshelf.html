Bookshelf

Every time I see someone (intellectually) interesting, I'm always curious in what books they read. However, by making this list, I'm not claiming as someone who are intellectually interesting.

I didn't finish reading everything listed here. I'm intrigued with the concept of anti-library that argues how unread books we own express things beyond our perimeter of knowledge.
